# Exploring the Voice Cast of Rick and Morty in 2025

![Exploring the Voice Cast of Rick and Morty in 2025](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/85eb0a0cf2c44dd58a10df4994880b98.jpg)Image Source: [pexels](<https://pexels.com>)

The world of _Rick and Morty_ has taken a bold new turn in 2025. Ian Cardoni and Harry Belden have stepped into the spotlight as the new rick and morty voice actors for Rick Sanchez and Morty Smith. Their arrival marks a fresh chapter for the show after Justin Roiland’s departure. Roiland, who had been the voice behind both characters, left following [allegations of domestic violence](<https://www.polygon.com/23652372/justin-roiland-response-domestic-violence-charges-dismissed>). This change shook the series but also opened doors for [deeper storytelling](<https://www.cracked.com/article_40270_the-new-rick-and-morty-voice-actors-are-better-than-justin-roiland-at-well-acting.html>) and fresh energy.

You might wonder how this shift impacts the show’s identity. Voice actors are the heart of animated series, and their performances breathe life into every character. Over the years, the rick and morty voice actors have shaped the show’s humor, drama, and emotional depth. With Cardoni and Belden bringing professionalism and commitment, fans can expect a new dynamic while still feeling the essence of the beloved characters.

## The Voices Behind Rick and Morty

![The Voices Behind Rick and Morty](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/52054a1e4f3c4c35b4e9dfd58e9cb91a.webp)Image Source: [pexels](<https://pexels.com>)

### Rick Sanchez

**Ian Cardoni**

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/b5590cfe5ec8485d9a261223672daf59-scaled.jpg)

You might be wondering, who is Ian Cardoni, and how does he fit into the shoes of Rick Sanchez? Well, Cardoni brings a fresh yet experienced voice to the table. His previous work includes roles in HBO’s _Clear History_ and narrating for WWE. These experiences have prepared him to tackle the complexity of Rick’s character.

> “Because I’m an actor by trade, I had that approach that I wanted to see if I could sustain it, see if I could take the character into new directions, and [I’m very grateful to step into the role](<https://www.cracked.com/article_44391_rick-and-morty-voice-actor-ian-cardoni-says-its-a-job-well-done-when-nobody-knows-who-he-is>),” Cardoni shared in an interview.

Rick Sanchez is no ordinary character. He’s the eccentric scientist who’s always one step ahead, whether he’s inventing mind-bending gadgets or outsmarting intergalactic foes. His chaotic genius and unpredictable nature make him the heart of the show. Cardoni’s ability to bring depth and nuance to his roles ensures that Rick’s sharp wit and emotional layers remain intact. You’ll still feel the same Rick you’ve grown to love, but with a hint of new energy.

### Morty Smith

**Harry Belden**

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/2156c171b97940f88775aad010bacef7-scaled.jpg)

Harry Belden steps in as the voice of Morty Smith, and he’s no stranger to the world of acting. You might recognize him from the Disney Channel original movie _Christmas Again_ (2021) or his appearances in _Chicago Med_ and _Joe Pera Talks With You_. His versatility as an actor makes him a perfect fit for Morty’s evolving character.

Morty isn’t just Rick’s naive grandson anymore. Over the seasons, he’s grown into a more complex character. He’s faced inter-dimensional chaos, navigated relationships, and even crossed paths with his darker counterpart, President Morty. Belden’s portrayal will need to capture this transformation. [Morty’s journey from a timid sidekick](<https://www.toonsmag.com/morty-smith/>) to a key player in the show’s overarching narrative is something fans eagerly follow. Belden’s fresh take promises to keep Morty’s charm alive while exploring new dimensions of his personality.

The main cast of _Rick and Morty_ has always been pivotal in shaping the series’ identity. With Ian Cardoni and Harry Belden joining the ranks of the rick and morty voice actors, the show is set to continue its legacy with a renewed vigor.

## Supporting Characters from Rick and Morty and Their Voice Actors

### Summer Smith

**Spencer Grammer**

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/806eabfaacc1447e833fcfd759a143bf.jpg)

Summer Smith has grown into one of the most dynamic characters in _Rick and Morty_. Initially, she seemed like your typical teenage sister, but over time, she’s proven to be so much more. Her relationships with Rick and her family have highlighted her resilience and adaptability.

Some of Summer’s most memorable moments include her quick thinking and bravery. For example:

  * In the episode _Morty’s Mind Blowers_ , she revives Rick and Morty after they’ve wiped their memories, showing her resourcefulness.

  * Her interactions with Rick often reveal her sharp wit and growing confidence.

Spencer Grammer’s voice work has been instrumental in making Summer a fan favorite. She brings a perfect balance of sarcasm, humor, and emotional depth to the character. You can feel Summer’s evolution through Grammer’s performance, making her a standout in the supporting cast.

### Beth Smith

**Sarah Chalke**

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/8e89f86e334c43cf820c3045b380eca7.jpg)

Beth Smith is one of the most complex characters in the series, and Sarah Chalke’s portrayal has played a huge role in shaping her. [Beth’s relationship with her father, Rick, is central to her character](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beth_Smith>). She idolizes him, even though his actions often hurt her. This dynamic creates a fascinating mix of admiration and resentment.

Chalke’s performance captures Beth’s internal struggles beautifully. She’s also brought depth to the introduction of Space Beth, a clone of Beth who adds another layer to her story. Chalke has expressed excitement about exploring how Beth’s childhood and her need for Rick’s approval influence her actions. This insight has helped make Beth one of the most intriguing members of the main cast.

### Jerry Smith

**Chris Parnell**

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/1c1a50195f9347efb35e5ef0c346ffae.jpg)

[Jerry Smith, voiced by Chris Parnell, is the show’s lovable loser](<https://decider.com/2020/05/14/rick-and-morty-chris-parnell-interview/>). He’s often portrayed as the “saddest man in the universe,” which makes his character both hilarious and relatable. Parnell’s comedic timing is spot-on, bringing Jerry’s awkwardness and vulnerability to life.

Some of Jerry’s funniest moments include:

  * His interactions with the Cenobites, where his cluelessness creates laugh-out-loud situations.

  * His rivalry-turned-friendship with Rick, which adds a surprising layer to his character.

Over the seasons, Jerry has grown in small but meaningful ways. He’s learned to accept the chaos of his life with Rick and has worked on improving his relationship with Beth. Parnell’s voice work ensures Jerry remains a vital part of the supporting cast, delivering both humor and heart.

### Birdperson

Here’s what makes Birdperson so compelling:

  * His caring nature shines through, especially when he expresses concern for Rick’s well-being.

Dan Harmon’s portrayal adds layers to Birdperson’s character. In _The Wedding Squanchers_ , you see the depth of their bond when Rick is devastated by Birdperson’s apparent death. Their friendship is a mix of loyalty, respect, and complexity, making Birdperson a vital part of the show’s emotional core.

## FAQ

### What inspired the casting changes in _Rick and Morty_?

The casting changes came after Justin Roiland’s departure. This shift gave the creators a chance to explore fresh voices and new dynamics for Rick and Morty. It’s all about keeping the show exciting and evolving for fans like you!

### Will the new voice actors sound like the original characters?

Yes, Ian Cardoni and Harry Belden aim to stay true to the original voices. They’ve worked hard to capture the essence of Rick and Morty while adding their own unique touch. You’ll notice the familiarity but also some refreshing nuances.

### Are the supporting characters’ voices changing too?

Nope! Spencer Grammer, Sarah Chalke, and Chris Parnell are still voicing Summer, Beth, and Jerry. Their performances continue to bring these beloved characters to life, keeping the family dynamic intact.

Expect thrilling adventures, deeper character arcs, and unexpected twists. The new voice cast and creative team are pushing boundaries to deliver episodes that’ll keep you hooked. As Ian Cardoni teased, “The best is yet to come!” 🚀
