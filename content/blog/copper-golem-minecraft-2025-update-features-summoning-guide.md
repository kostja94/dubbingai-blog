### Oxidation and Waxing

Copper gear changes over time because of oxidation. The surface of copper reacts with air and turns green. Players can [clean copper gear by polishing it](<https://www.canada.ca/en/conservation-institute/services/conservation-preservation-publications/canadian-conservation-institute-notes/care--brass-copper.html>). Cleaning removes dirt and tarnish. Polishing uses gentle abrasives to keep the surface smooth. Waxing is the best way to protect copper gear. Wax forms a thin layer that blocks air and slows down oxidation. Players can remove the wax with mineral spirits if they want to clean the gear again. These steps help players keep their copper gear looking new. The copper update makes copper more valuable in minecraft because players can use it for strong gear and stylish storage.

> Tip: Wax your copper gear and chests to keep them shiny and bright for a long time.
