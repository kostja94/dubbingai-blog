## Best Free VTuber Creation Software

### VRoid Studio

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/94d27de2e1ac40d9865e8c038a083c2f.png)

[VRoid Studio](<https://vroid.com/en/studio>) is great for making free VTuber avatars. Beginners and experts both love it. Why? It’s easy to use and lets you design cool 3D avatars for free. You can change hairstyles, faces, and clothes to fit your style.

One awesome thing about VRoid Studio is its online community. There are many tutorials, tips, and free assets shared by users. This makes learning avatar creation much easier, even if you’re new. But remember, it needs a strong computer. If your PC is slow, it might lag.

### Live2D Cubism

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/bec0d3dcf6414745acfdad66b2ba4ecc.jpg)

If you like 2D avatars, Live2D Cubism is amazing. It’s one of the top tools for making anime-style 2D models. Unlike 3D tools, this focuses on a unique anime look.

Live2D Cubism is [budget-friendly](<https://vtubersensei.com/2024/10/01/live2d-vs-3d-vtubing-which-is-best-for-you/>). You only need a webcam and software like VTube Studio to use it. Its smart features make animating and rigging faster. You can finish a 2D avatar in weeks, not months.

Advantage| Description  
---|---  
Budget-friendly| Costs less than making and rigging 3D models.  
Easy for beginners| Needs simple hardware and is easy to learn.  
Anime-style look| Loved by anime fans for its special design.  
Quick to finish| Models take less time compared to 3D ones.  
  
### Waidayo

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/7934747453e04ee39d0e18c70bb25072.jpg)

Waidayo is a cool free app for VTubers. It connects your phone to your computer and turns your phone into a motion tracker. This is great for beginners who don’t want to buy pricey gear.

The app is easy to set up and works with many VTuber tools. It helps capture facial expressions and head movements, making your avatar more lifelike. While it doesn’t have advanced features, it’s simple and perfect for new users.

## Free Face Tracking Software for Beginner VTubers

### VSeeFace

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/8372e96be6db4d4090b41beac8623d7f.png)

[VSeeFace](<https://www.vseeface.icu/>) is a popular free tool for face tracking. It’s [open-source and has many features to make avatars lively](<https://geekflare.com/best-vtuber-software/>). You can track your face and hands for more realistic movements. The software uses smart tech to [detect expressions in real-time](<https://vtuberart.com/top-10-best-vtuber-maker-software-2022/>). It captures details like blinking, eyebrow raises, and mouth positions. This makes your avatar look natural and fun to watch.

Many VTubers like VSeeFace because it’s simple and accurate. You only need a webcam to start using it. Whether you smile or frown, it shows your expressions perfectly. If you want a dependable tool for VTubing, this is a great pick.

### Animaze

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/8ce43651f7994445b97f126267ee7cfa.jpg)

[Animaze](<https://www.animaze.us/>) is another free face-tracking app for VTubers. It’s easy to use and works with many types of avatars. But, it has some downsides. For example, [the lip-syncing isn’t always perfect](<https://www.rokoko.com/insights/best-facerig-alternative-for-vtubers-live>). Some users said their avatar’s mouth stayed open even when it shouldn’t. Also, Animaze only tracks faces, not upper body movements.

Even with these issues, Animaze is good for beginners. It’s quick to set up and lets you try different avatars for free.

### Kalidoface 3D

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/24cecc15e60f4836ae7b23c807335778.jpg)

[Kalidoface 3D](<https://3d.kalidoface.com/>) is a face-tracking tool you can use in your browser. You don’t need to download anything—just open it and start. It tracks your face and moves your avatar in real-time. This makes it a handy choice for beginners who want something simple.

One great thing about Kalidoface 3D is how easy it is to access. You can use it on almost any device, even phones. While it doesn’t have advanced features, it’s a great tool for starting your VTubing journey.

## Free Streaming and Broadcasting Tools

### OBS Studio

OBS Studio is a top choice for VTuber streaming. It’s free, open-source, and full of helpful features. You can record, stream, and edit your VTuber content easily. The best thing? It runs smoothly without slowing your computer.

This tool lets you control your streams completely. You can add scenes, switch between them, and include cool overlays or animations. It’s great for VTubers who want professional-looking streams without spending money.

But OBS Studio can be tricky to learn at first. If you’re new, it might take time to understand all the settings. Don’t worry—there are many online tutorials to guide you. Once you learn it, you’ll see why so many VTubers love using it.

### Streamlabs

Streamlabs is another great tool for VTuber streaming. It’s made to help your streams look polished and fun. Unlike OBS Studio, Streamlabs is easier to use. It’s perfect for beginners starting their VTubing journey.

One cool feature of Streamlabs is its built-in alerts. You can add notifications for followers, donations, or subscribers right into your stream. This makes your content more exciting for viewers.

However, Streamlabs uses more computer power than OBS Studio. If your computer is older, it might run slower. Here’s a quick comparison to help you choose:

Feature| Streamlabs| OBS Studio  
---|---|---  
Price| Free (extra features cost)| Free  
User interface| Simple and easy to use| Harder to learn  
Customization| Less flexible than OBS| Very customizable  
Built-in alerts| Yes| No (needs plugins)  
Resource usage| [Higher than OBS](<https://www.fastpix.io/blog/streamlabs-vs-obs-studio>)| Lower than Streamlabs

If you want an all-in-one streaming tool, Streamlabs is a solid pick. Just make sure your computer can handle its higher resource needs.

> **Tip:** OBS Studio is better for advanced users who want more control. Streamlabs is great for beginners who like simple tools.

## FAQ

### What is a VTuber?

A VTuber is someone who uses a digital avatar to make videos or stream. They play games, chat, or share fun content with their viewers.

### Do I need expensive gear to start VTubing?

No, you don’t need pricey gear! [Free apps like VRoid Studio](<https://dubbingai.io/blog/free-rizz-soundboards-online-tips/>), VSeeFace, and OBS Studio help you start. All you need is a computer and a webcam.

### Can I use these tools on any computer?

Most tools work on regular computers. But some, like VRoid Studio, need [stronger hardware](<https://dubbingai.io/blog/voxal-voice-changer-safety-reliability/>). Always check the system needs before installing.
