Reyna’s calm yet intimidating delivery is key to her persona. She doesn’t shout or panic; instead, she speaks with a composed tone that unnerves her enemies. To replicate this, keep your voice steady and controlled. Imagine you’re delivering a warning rather than a threat. For example, her ultimate ability’s fierce cry signals her readiness to dominate, but it’s not chaotic—it’s deliberate and powerful.

Her vampiric abilities, like Devour, come with chilling sound effects that add to her menacing aura. You can mirror this by maintaining a low, steady pitch when speaking. This approach creates an air of mystery and danger, making you sound more like Reyna.
