## Safety and legal tips

### Copyright check

Before you [download any oof sound effect](<https://dubbingai.io/blog/return-by-death-sound-effect-download/>), you should always check if you have the right to use it. Some sounds are free for everyone, but others may need permission or credit. Look for a license or usage note on the website. If you see “public domain” or “Creative Commons Zero,” you can use the sound without worry. If the site asks you to give credit, make sure you do that in your project. When in doubt, pick another sound or ask the creator.

> Tip: Sites like Pixabay and Freesound show license info right next to each sound. Always read it before you download.

### Avoiding malware

You want your device to stay safe. Some sites may try to trick you with fake download buttons or pop-up ads. Only click the real download button. If a site asks you to install extra software, close the page. Use an ad blocker if you can. Trusted sites like Myinstants, Audio.com, and YouTube Audio Library rarely have these problems.

Here’s a quick checklist to help you avoid malware:

  * Download only from trusted sites.

  * Never open strange files or .exe downloads.

  * Scan every file with your antivirus before using it.

### Safe download practices

You can keep your downloads safe by following a few simple steps. Always double-check the file type. Most oof sound effect files come as MP3 or WAV. If you see a different file type, be careful. Save your files in a folder you trust. Use tools like Dubbing AI to edit or manage your sounds. Dubbing AI works online, so you don’t need to install anything risky.
