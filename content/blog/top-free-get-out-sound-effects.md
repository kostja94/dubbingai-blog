## How to Use Free Sound Effects Safely

### Knowing Licensing and Giving Credit

It’s important to know about licensing when using free sounds. Some sites give royalty-free sounds, but others need credit. For example, [Free Music Archive and SoundsCrate might ask you to credit the creator.](<https://www.b2w.tv/blog/best-free-sound-effects-sites>) On the other hand, Pixabay and Mixkit let you use sounds without giving credit.

Source| License Type| Credit Needed| Can Use for Business?  
---|---|---|---  
Free Music Archive| Creative Commons| Sometimes| Yes  
SoundsCrate| Varies| Sometimes| Yes  
Pixabay| Pixabay License| No| Yes  
Mixkit| Mixkit License| No| Yes  
99Sounds| Royalty-Free| No| Yes  
  
Always read the license rules before downloading. This helps you follow the rules and avoid problems.

### Staying Away from Copyright Issues

[Using copyrighted sounds can cause big problems.](<https://gyre.pro/blog/top--websites-with-free-sound-effects>) YouTube might remove your video or block your account if you break rules often. To stay safe:

  * [Use sounds from trusted websites.](<https://uppbeat.io/blog/sound-effects/free-sound-effects-websites>)

  * Choose royalty-free sound effects.

  * Don’t use copyrighted audio to avoid strikes.

  * Check the rules for platforms like TikTok and Instagram.

### Tips for Giving Proper Credit

If credit is needed, make sure to do it correctly. Add the creator’s name in your credits or notes. Follow the license rules to avoid trouble. Giving credit also helps support sound creators.
