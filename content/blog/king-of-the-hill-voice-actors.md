### Hank Hill and Boomhauer

When you think of “King of the Hill,” Hank Hill’s voice stands out. His calm and steady tone fits a hardworking Texan who loves propane. Mike Judge, the voice actor for Hank, didn’t stop there. He also voiced Boomhauer, the neighbor who talks super fast and is hard to understand but funny.

Judge’s skill in voicing two very different characters is amazing. Hank is serious and practical, while Boomhauer is odd and mysterious. Both feel real because of Judge’s talent. You can almost hear Hank saying, “That boy ain’t right,” or Boomhauer’s quick chatter like they’re real people.

## Pamela Adlon: Bobby Hill’s Voice

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/9ccbff8fa49545c89a0af6ef63451467.jpg)

### Bobby Hill’s Role in the Show

Bobby Hill is not like most TV kids. He’s funny, creative, and full of surprises. As Hank’s son, he often questions his dad’s old-fashioned ideas. Bobby doesn’t care about sports or propane. Instead, he loves acting, comedy, and trying new things. His curiosity and innocence make him easy to relate to, especially as he deals with growing up.

You might remember Bobby’s funny lines, like “That’s my purse! I don’t know you!” These moments show how unique and lovable he is. Whether he’s doing stand-up comedy or hanging out with Bill, Bobby always brings something fresh to the show. His character adds both humor and heart, making him a fan favorite in _King of the Hill_.

### Pamela Adlon’s Career and Accolades

[Pamela Adlon](<https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0781899/>)’s work as Bobby Hill’s voice is amazing. She even won a [2002 Emmy for Outstanding Voice Over](<https://kingofthehill.fandom.com/wiki/King_of_the_Hill>) because of it. Her talent made Bobby’s innocence and humor unforgettable.

Adlon’s career includes much more than _King of the Hill_. She created and starred in _Better Things_ , a hit show about family and life. This show shows her skills in acting and storytelling. Whether voicing Bobby or acting in dramas, Adlon proves she can do it all.

### Her Contributions to ‘King of the Hill’

Pamela Adlon didn’t just voice Bobby; she made him real. Her acting gave Bobby his humor, kindness, and depth. This made him one of the most relatable characters on the show.

Her work also made Bobby famous worldwide. Fans loved his quirky charm, similar to Bart Simpson from _The Simpsons_. Adlon’s talent turned Bobby into a cultural icon. Without her, Bobby wouldn’t have been the same.

Adlon’s impact went beyond Bobby. Her skill inspired the cast and improved the show’s stories. Whether Bobby was learning from Hank or joking with Bill, Adlon’s voice made every scene special.

Her relationship with Lucky adds more to her story as she balances love and finding herself. Episodes like “Boxing Luanne” and “Pigmalion” show her hidden talents and intelligence. She proves she’s more than just her looks.

Fans love these things about Luanne:

  * How she grows from a naive girl to a strong woman.

  * Her funny and unique personality.

  * Her ability to surprise with her strength and depth.

Luanne Platter isn’t just a character; she shows how people grow and find themselves.

## Supporting Cast and Guest Stars in ‘King of the Hill’

### Stephen Root as Bill Dauterive and Buck Strickland

Stephen Root voiced two memorable characters in _King of the Hill_. Bill Dauterive is Hank’s lonely but lovable friend. He’s always looking for love but often messes up. Root’s voice makes Bill’s sadness and humor feel real.

Buck Strickland, Hank’s boss, is the opposite of Hank. He’s lazy, dishonest, and loves taking shortcuts. Root switches easily between Bill’s soft voice and Buck’s loud arrogance. This shows how talented he is as a voice actor.

Root’s career goes beyond this show. He’s acted in _Office Space_ , _Barry_ , and _Get Out_. His ability to play many roles makes him a respected actor.

> Fun Fact: Stephen Root has voiced over 200 characters in his career!

### Johnny Hardwick as Dale Gribble

Dale Gribble is one of the funniest characters in _King of the Hill_. He believes in wild conspiracies and loves pest control. Johnny Hardwick gave Dale his unique voice, making him unforgettable.

Dale’s actions always make people laugh. Whether he’s talking about secret government plans or outsmarting his wife Nancy, he steals the spotlight. Hardwick’s voice adds charm to Dale’s quirky personality.

Hardwick also helped write for the show. His ideas made Dale’s funniest moments even better.

> Dale Gribble’s catchphrase, “Sh-sh-sha!” is one of the show’s most famous lines.

### Toby Huss as Kahn Souphanousinphone and Cotton Hill

Toby Huss voiced two very different characters in the show. Kahn Souphanousinphone, Hank’s sarcastic neighbor, loves to brag. His rivalry with Hank and sharp humor make him stand out. Huss’s voice makes Kahn’s arrogance funny and enjoyable.

Cotton Hill, Hank’s tough dad, is another character Huss voiced. Cotton’s rough voice and wild war stories make him unforgettable. Huss’s ability to switch between Kahn’s wit and Cotton’s gruffness shows his skill.

Huss has also worked on other shows like _The Adventures of Pete & Pete_ and _Halt and Catch Fire_. But his work on _King of the Hill_ is a big highlight.

> Did you know? Toby Huss made up many of Cotton Hill’s funniest lines on the spot!
