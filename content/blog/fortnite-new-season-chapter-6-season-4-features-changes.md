# All New Features and Changes in Fortnite Chapter 6 Season 4

![All New Features and Changes in Fortnite Chapter 6 Season 4](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/b463017ec43245d1b73aa58683570e8f.jpg)Image Source: [unsplash](<https://unsplash.com>)

You are starting the Fortnite new season, and it is full of excitement. Chapter 6 season 4, known as “Shock ‘N Awesome,” brings fresh map locations, wild weapons, and major changes to gameplay. In this Fortnite new season, you’ll encounter [pets that follow you and can use Shadow Stones to turn invisible](<https://www.fortnite.com/patch-notes/v6-00>). Explore new areas like the Floating Island and Haunted Castle. [More than 14 million players](<https://icon-era.com/blog/fortnite-live-player-count-and-statistics.76/>) joined the Fortnite new season launch for chapter 6 season 4, highlighting how massive this update is.

Every Fortnite new season feels unique, but Fortnite chapter 6 stands out with its infestation theme, revamped map, and exciting new features.

## Key Takeaways

  * Fortnite Chapter 6 Season 4 has a bug infestation theme. There are new map places like The Hive and Swarmy Stash. These places give you new spots to explore and fight.

  * New weapons include the Leadspitter 3000 minigun and the Roly Poly Transformation item. These items let you fight bugs and other players in fun ways.

  * The season brings a new Boons and Medallions system. This system gives you strong buffs. These buffs do not use up inventory space. It changes how you play the game.

  * Vehicles like the Hammerhead Choppa and the Roly Poly Vehicle are here. They help you move faster and fight in new ways. This makes matches quicker and more exciting.

  * The Battle Pass has cool crossovers with Halo and Power Rangers. You can also get special skins and rewards as you play this season.

## Fortnite Chapter 6 Season 4 Gameplay Changes

### Boons & Medallions

You get to try out a brand-new system this season: Boons and Medallions. These [special items](<https://game8.co/games/Fortnite/archives/487337>) give you powerful buffs that can change the way you play. You don’t have to worry about losing inventory space, because medallions work passively once you pick them up. You earn them by defeating tough bosses around the map. Each medallion gives you a unique bonus, so you can build your strategy around the one you find.

Here’s a quick look at some of the medallions and what they do:

Medallion Name| Benefit Provided| How to Obtain  
---|---|---  
Shogun X Medallion| Grants infinite stamina and cloaking while sprinting| Defeat the Shogun X boss at Shogun’s Arena  
Night Rose Medallion| Weapons automatically reload ammo continuously| Defeat the Night Rose boss in Demon’s Dojo  
  
You can use these medallions to sprint forever, stay hidden, or never worry about reloading. This makes every fight feel different. You might rush into battle with infinite stamina, or sneak up on enemies while cloaked. The right medallion can turn the tide of a match.

### O.X.R. Rank System

Fortnite chapter 6 season 4 introduces the O.X.R. Rank System. This new ranking system tracks your progress and matches you with players at your skill level. You start at the lower ranks and work your way up by winning matches and completing challenges. The higher your rank, the tougher your opponents get. You can see your rank badge next to your name, so everyone knows how far you’ve come.

The O.X.R. Rank System also unlocks special rewards as you climb. You might get exclusive skins, emotes, or even bonus XP. This system keeps every match exciting, because you always have something to work toward. If you love competition, you’ll want to push for the top ranks.

### Bug Bosses & Enemies

Get ready for some wild fights against giant bugs and new enemy types. The infestation theme brings bug bosses to the island, and each one has its own attack style. You’ll find these bosses in secret areas or at high bug activity zones. Defeating them is tough, but you get big rewards like medallions, mythic weapons, and rare loot.

You also face swarms of smaller enemies. Some bugs burrow underground, while others fly or spit acid. You need to use new weapons and items to survive. The [Swarmstrike rocket launcher](<https://egw.news/gaming/news/29065/fortnite-unveils-swarmstrike-rocket-launcher-for-c-3qGOXegrQ>) helps clear out groups of bugs fast. Mud sliding returns this season, letting you move quickly and hide from thermal sights. Sliding through mud gives you a speed boost and makes you harder to spot for about 30 seconds. This mechanic lets you rush enemies or sneak past bug swarms, so you can play aggressive or stealthy.

  * Mud sliding gives you a [speed boost and thermal stealth for 30 seconds](<https://beebom.com/fortnite-is-turning-back-the-clock-on-this-iconic-mechanic-in-next-season/>).

  * You can use mud to escape bug bosses or surprise other players.

  * The mud effect wears off if you jump in water or after time runs out.

> Callout: Always look for muddy areas when you see lots of bug activity. You’ll get a big advantage in both movement and stealth!

### Blitz Royale & Tournament Updates

Fortnite shakes up the competitive scene with new Blitz Royale playlists and tournament formats. Blitz Royale now comes in [three wild versions](<https://fortnitetracker.com/article/2379/fortnite-36-30-update>):

  * **Six Stack:** Teams of six battle it out in a speedy Blitz format.

  * **Six Stack (Pure Chaos):** Six-player teams, Siphon, and no bots for total chaos.

You’ll notice some map changes in Blitz, like Retail Row getting swapped out for MEGA City. If you win five Blitz Royale matches or level up your account five times, you unlock a free back bling. This event runs until August 12, so jump in soon!

Tournaments also get a big refresh in chapter 6 season 4. You can join [Cash Cups, Victory Cash Cups, Open Cups, and Cosmetic Cups](<https://fortnite.fandom.com/wiki/Tournament>). Each one has its own rules and rewards. Some tournaments use special rules like Siphon, faster material harvesting, or moving storm circles. You need to meet certain rank requirements to enter, like Bronze I for FNCS Community Cup or Gold I for Victory Cash Cups.

Here’s what’s new in the tournament scene:

  * Scheduled sessions where everyone starts equally and earns points for eliminations and survival.

  * [Reload Quick Cups](<https://www.fortnite.com/news/fortnite-br-chapter-6-season-4-competitive-details-claim-your-place-on-champions-road>) with multiple rounds and capped eliminations.

  * Duos Divisional Cups now have more divisions and faster promotions.

  * Solo Series lets you qualify for finals by playing your best four out of six weeks.

  * Champions Road Tournaments offer special events and unique cosmetic rewards.

You can watch live matches or replays using the in-game Replay Client. All tournament info is in the “Compete” tab, with events running from August 12 to September 7.

## Vehicles & Aerial Combat

### Hammerhead Choppa

You’ll spot the Hammerhead Choppa flying over the island this season. This [helicopter-like vehicle](<https://gonintendo.com/contents/51536-fortnite-chapter-6-season-4-shock-n-awesome-goes-live-today>) changes how you move and fight in Fortnite Chapter 6 Season 4. This cannon lets you lay down heavy fire on enemy squads or bug swarms. The Choppa’s mix of flying and shooting makes it perfect for quick escapes or surprise attacks. You can use it to reach high places, chase down opponents, or rain down shots from above. It’s not just about speed—this vehicle lets you surprise your opponents and break through their builds. If you want to try something new, grab a Roly Poly and roll right into the action.

## FAQ

### How do you find the new Roly Poly Transformation item?

You can find the Roly Poly Transformation item in rare loot chests or by defeating bug bosses. Look for glowing chests in secret areas or check high bug activity zones. Try using it right away for a wild ride!

### What is the fastest way to level up your battle pass?

Play daily quests and complete weekly challenges. Join Blitz Royale matches for bonus XP. Team up with friends to finish group tasks faster. You can also buy levels with V-Bucks if you want to unlock rewards quickly.

### Can you play as the Megazord in every match?

No, you can only play as the Megazord during special limited-time events. Watch for event dates in the game news. Complete Megazord quests to unlock the skin and join the event when it goes live.

### Where do you find bug bosses on the map?

Bug bosses spawn in secret areas and at new POIs like The Hive or Swarmy Stash. Look for big bug nests or listen for loud bug sounds. Defeating a boss gives you mythic loot and medallions.
