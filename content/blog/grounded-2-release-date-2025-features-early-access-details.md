## Pre-Order Bonuses and Special Editions

Players can pick from two editions of Grounded 2. The choices are the [Standard Edition and the Founder’s Edition](<https://insider-gaming.com/all-grounded-2-pre-order-bonuses-editions/>). Each one gives special bonuses. These bonuses make the game more fun but do not change how you play.

Edition| Price| Included Exclusive Content  
---|---|---  
Standard Edition| $29.99 (or free with Game Pass)| Tools of Yore Bundle (cosmetic set available to all players)  
Founder’s Edition| $49.99| Tools of Yore Bundle, four premium character skins, ONYX-PRO digital artbook and soundtrack, secret items  
  
The Standard Edition lets you play the whole game. It also gives you the Tools of Yore Bundle. This bundle has items that let you change how your character looks. Everyone gets these items, no matter which edition they buy. If you have Game Pass, you get the Standard Edition for free.

The Founder’s Edition has everything in the Standard Edition. It also gives you more digital rewards. You get four special character skins. These skins let you change your avatar’s look. The ONYX-PRO digital artbook and soundtrack show how the game was made. There are also secret items that will be shown later. These surprises are for early fans. All Founder’s Edition rewards are just for looks. They do not help you win.

> If you start with Game Pass, you can upgrade to the Founder’s Edition later. This means everyone can get the special items if they want.

Here are some important things about pre-ordering Grounded 2:

Players can choose the edition they like best. Both editions let you play the full game. The Founder’s Edition is good for fans who want more special items.
