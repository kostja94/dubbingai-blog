## Tips for Safe Downloading

Getting sound effects can be fun, but [safety matters](<https://dubbingai.io/blog/why-does-helium-change-your-voice-safety-tips/>). Follow these tips to keep your downloads safe and easy.

### Check File Formats

Always check the file type before downloading. Some formats are better than others. For clear sound, choose **AIFF** or **WAV** files. These keep the original sound quality and work with most editing tools. They’re great for high-quality projects. Formats like **MP3** are okay for casual use but may lose sound details.

> **Pro Tip:** Listen to the sound first to make sure it’s good.

### Stay Away from Suspicious Links

Some websites have fake download buttons. Don’t click them! Use [trusted sites](<https://dubbingai.io/blog/voxal-voice-changer-safety-reliability/>) like **Freesound** or **Zapsplat** for safe downloads. If a link looks strange or sends you to another page, close it. Scammers use fake links to spread harmful files.

  * **Things to avoid:**

    * Pop-ups asking for private info.

    * Links that don’t match the site’s name.

    * Files with odd endings like `.exe` for audio.

### Install Antivirus Software

Even safe sites can have risks. Antivirus software protects your device by checking files for threats. It works quietly in the background, keeping you safe.

> 🛡️ **Tip:** Update your antivirus often to block new dangers.
