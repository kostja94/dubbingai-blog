### Understanding Creative Commons licenses

Creative Commons licenses can seem tricky, but they’re actually pretty simple. These licenses tell you how you can use a sound. For example:

  * **CC0 (Public Domain):** You can use the sound however you want, no credit needed.

  * **CC BY:** You can use it, but you must give credit to the creator.

  * **CC BY-NC:** You can use it for personal projects, but not for commercial ones.

Always read the license type carefully. It’s the best way to avoid legal trouble later.

### Red flags to watch out for when downloading sound samples

Not all websites are trustworthy. Watch out for these warning signs:
