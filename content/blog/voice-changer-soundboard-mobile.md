## Tips for Optimizing Performance

### Reducing Latency and Improving Audio Quality

#### Closing background apps to free up resources

Your mobile device runs multiple apps simultaneously, which can slow down performance. To ensure smooth operation of your Dubbing AI app, close unnecessary background apps. These apps consume memory and processing power, which can lead to latency issues. By freeing up resources, you allow the app to process your voice changes and sound effects faster. This step is especially important if you’re using [real-time features](<https://elevenlabs.io/blog/the-best-voice-changer-for-skype>) like live voice modulation or soundboards during calls or gaming sessions.

#### Using headphones for better sound clarity

Headphones can significantly improve your audio experience. Built-in phone speakers often lack the clarity needed for precise voice modulation. Using headphones ensures you hear every detail of your voice changes and sound effects. They also help reduce background noise, making your recordings or live interactions sound more professional. For the best results, choose headphones with a built-in microphone. This setup enhances both input and output quality, giving you a seamless dubbing experience.
