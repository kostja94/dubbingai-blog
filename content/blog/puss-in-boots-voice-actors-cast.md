# Meet the Voice Cast of Puss in Boots: The Last Wish

![Meet the Voice Cast of Puss in Boots The Last Wish](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/40b7c0d1254e442aaf7b4c5c18391ecd.jpg)Image Source: [unsplash](<https://unsplash.com>)

Have you ever wondered what makes “Puss in Boots: The Last Wish” so captivating? It’s not just the story—it’s the incredible voice cast! With a [worldwide gross of $485.3 million and a 95% Rotten Tomatoes rating](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puss_in_Boots:_The_Last_Wish>), this movie shines. Each Puss in Boots voice actor brings their character to life with unforgettable charm.

## Key Takeaways

  * Antonio Banderas has been Puss in Boots’s voice for 18 years. He made the character loved in the _Shrek_ movies.

  * Salma Hayek Pinault adds fun and personality to Kitty Softpaws. She makes Puss’s adventure even more exciting.

  * New characters like Florence Pugh’s Goldilocks and Harvey Guillén’s Perrito bring new life and meaning to the story.

## The Returning Stars

### Antonio Banderas as Puss in Boots

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/eef4873e1080414c98c445ab2e64b0fa.jpg)

When you think of Puss in Boots, you likely hear Antonio Banderas’s voice. He has been voicing Puss since _Shrek 2_ in 2004. Over time, he turned Puss from a sidekick into a main character. Did you know he’s [voiced Puss for over 18 years](<https://ew.com/movies/antonio-banderas-explains-puss-in-boots-history-shrek/?srsltid=AfmBOop8FPBVJ56pMEfHzWZvR_WMyDK8Ls6pC3g1YHj7mVC-XxhQKsB1>)? That makes him the longest-running character in the _Shrek_ series!

Banderas says Puss feels like his “other self.” Puss’s bravery and mischief reflect parts of Banderas’s personality. You might see similarities between Puss and Banderas’s roles like Zorro or his _Desperado_ character. Critics love how he adds charm and boldness to Puss, making him unforgettable. For Banderas, voicing Puss is both a challenge and a proud moment. He also enjoys representing Puss’s Latino roots.

### Salma Hayek Pinault as Kitty Softpaws

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/bbd1657b8c0f4e4f8f7d4b7afc679642.jpg)

Salma Hayek Pinault is back as Kitty Softpaws, [Puss’s old love and sometimes rival](<https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/lists/puss-in-boots-the-last-wish-animated-character-antonio-banderas-salma-hayek-florence-pugh/>). Kitty makes _The Last Wish_ more exciting by helping Puss on his journey. Hayek’s voice acting stands out because she mixes humor, charm, and sass.

Hayek first played Kitty in the 2011 _Puss in Boots_ movie, which earned [$554.9 million](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salma_Hayek>). She returned for _The Last Wish_ , another box office success. Here’s a quick look at her work:

Movie Title| Role| Box Office Earnings| Oscar Nomination  
---|---|---|---  
 _Puss in Boots_ (2011)| Kitty Softpaws| $554.9 million| Nominated for Best Animated Film  
 _Puss in Boots: The Last Wish_|  Kitty Softpaws| $485.3 million| Nominated for Best Animated Film  
  
Hayek and Banderas have worked together for years, starting with _Desperado_. Their teamwork shines in _The Last Wish_ , making their scenes fun to watch. Hayek once joked about “riding” Banderas’s fame, but she clearly brings her own style to Kitty Softpaws.

## The New Additions

### Florence Pugh as Goldilocks

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/449876defe4840d8954050b1b1a9a308-scaled.jpg)

Florence Pugh voices [Goldilocks](<https://shrek.fandom.com/wiki/Goldilocks>), and she does an amazing job. You might know her from movies like _Little Women_ or _Midsommar_. In this film, she gives Goldilocks a new twist. Goldilocks isn’t just curious anymore—she’s part of a bear family on an adventure.

> [Pugh works well with Ray Winstone](<https://www.siftpop.com/puss-in-boots-the-last-wish-movie-review/>), who voices Papa Bear. Their teamwork adds emotion to the story, making their scenes memorable.

Her Goldilocks performance mixes humor and heart, showing why she’s a rising star.

### Harvey Guillén as Perrito

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/4f0a5e89d60d4eb2b5d6ed4cfb4f0221.png)

Harvey Guillén voices Perrito, a funny and lovable dog in the movie. Perrito is small but has a big heart and endless optimism.

> Guillén makes Perrito funny without being annoying. His sad backstory is told in a way that’s both hilarious and touching.

Perrito brings laughs and supports Puss and Kitty, making him a standout character.

### John Mulaney as “Big” Jack Horner

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/c7a586d54bb04d1091c7a589eb82ddda-scaled.jpg)

John Mulaney voices “Big” Jack Horner, a funny and over-the-top villain. His performance is inspired by his love for stand-up comedy.

  * Mulaney’s style is influenced by comedians like Chris Rock and Jerry Seinfeld.

  * His timing and humor make Jack Horner both funny and scary.

Jack Horner’s wild personality adds chaos and fun to the movie.

### Wagner Moura as The Big Bad Wolf

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/586bd434ec684545b4fecba7509b0bf1.jpg)

Wagner Moura voices the Big Bad Wolf, a truly scary villain. Unlike past _Shrek_ villains, this Wolf is serious and terrifying.

  1. His creepy whistle and glowing red eyes make him unforgettable.

  2. The fight scenes between Puss and the Wolf are intense and exciting.

  3. Moura’s voice makes the Wolf even more menacing.

The Big Bad Wolf isn’t just a bad guy—he’s a powerful force in the story.

## Other Notable Voices from Puss in Boots: The Last Wish

### Kevin McCann as Ethical Bug

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/1c6c013e0dbe4d73a4b4ba59c9b314e7.jpg)

[Kevin McCann voices the Ethical Bug](<https://hero.fandom.com/wiki/Ethical_Bug>), making him memorable and unique. His voice sounds like Jimmy Stewart, a famous old-time actor. This fits because the Bug is Jack Horner’s moral guide.

The Bug’s part is small but very important. He tries to help Jack change, but Jack stays bad. The Bug even calls Jack an “irredeemable monster.” In one key moment, the Bug burns the Wishing Star Map. This stops Jack from getting ultimate power. McCann’s acting makes the Bug tiny but unforgettable.

### Conrad Vernon as Gingy

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/13c5efdd6d5f44689376f30bf2e1af5a.jpg)

[Conrad Vernon is back as Gingy](<https://www.polygon.com/movies/22434573/gingerbread-man-shrek-torture-scene>), the funny Gingerbread Man. Did you know Gingy’s voice started as a joke? Vernon copied a kid’s voice he once babysat. That voice became famous, and fans love Gingy for it.

Vernon does more than just voice Gingy. He helped create the _Shrek_ world by drawing storyboards and directing parts of the series. His work shaped the franchise, and Gingy adds humor and charm to _Puss in Boots: The Last Wish_.

> “Not the gumdrop buttons!”—Gingy’s line still makes everyone laugh!

### Cody Cameron as Pinocchio

![](https://dubbingai.io/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/824f572fdcab4e7ca4fa5fc3dbb36bad.jpg)

## FAQ

### What makes “Puss in Boots: The Last Wish” different from the first movie?

This sequel dives deeper into Puss’s character. You’ll see him face his fears, reflect on his life, and embrace a more emotional journey.

### Can kids enjoy this movie, or is it more for adults?

It’s perfect for all ages! Kids love the humor and action, while adults appreciate the deeper themes and clever storytelling.

> **Tip** : Watch it with your family for a fun movie night!

### Is it necessary to watch the first “Puss in Boots” movie before this one?

Not at all! The story stands on its own. But if you’ve seen the first, you’ll enjoy the returning characters even more.
