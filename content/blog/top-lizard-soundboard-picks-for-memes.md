## FAQ

### How do I add lizard sounds to my meme videos?

You can download the sound from a [soundboard site](<https://dubbingai.io/blog/peewee-purpdrank-soundboard-app-features-user-experience-review/>). Then, use a video editor like CapCut or InShot. Just import your video, add the sound, and adjust the timing. That’s it!

### Are lizard soundboards free to use?

Most lizard soundboards offer free sounds. Some sites may have premium features, but you can find plenty of great options without paying. Always check the site’s terms before using sounds in your content.

### Can I use lizard sounds on TikTok or Instagram?

Yes! Download the sound and upload it to your video in the app’s editor. You can also use trending lizard sounds from TikTok’s audio library. Just tap “Add Sound” and search for lizard noises.

### What if I want a custom lizard sound?

> Many soundboards let you upload your own audio. Record your lizard sound, save it as an MP3 or WAV file, and add it to your board. Now you have a unique sound for your memes!
