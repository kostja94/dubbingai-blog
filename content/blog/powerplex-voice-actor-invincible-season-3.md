## Powerplex in Invincible: A New Villain Appears

### Powerplex’s Past and Reasons for Revenge

Powerplex isn’t like other villains. His story starts with sadness and loss, making him one of the most emotional characters in _Invincible_. Before he became Powerplex, he worked at the Pentagon. [His life changed forever when his sister and niece died](<https://www.yahoo.com/entertainment/powerplex-voices-him-invincible-powers-153356425.html>) during a fight involving Mark Grayson, also known as Invincible. This tragic event broke his heart.

Imagine losing loved ones in such a terrible way. For Powerplex, this pain turned into anger. He blames Grayson for their deaths and uses his grief to fuel his mission for revenge. His backstory shows he’s not just a bad guy but someone driven by deep emotions. This makes him stand out in the series.

### Powerplex’s Special Skills and Strengths

Powerplex has impressive powers. [He can control energy and turn it into electricity](<https://www.superherohype.com/guides/594102-invincible-season-3-powerplex-powers-abilities-voice-aaron-paul-cast>), a skill called electrokinesis. This ability lets him shoot electric blasts and even fly. But using too much energy tires him out, so he has to be careful.

He’s also very tough. He can survive hits that would hurt most people. Plus, he uses stolen Bio-Therm discs to boost his powers. These discs help him control his energy better, making him a strong enemy. His powers aren’t just about strength—they require smart thinking, which makes him even more interesting.
