Marge Simpson’s voice actor is Julie Kavner, who began voicing Marge when The Simpsons first appeared on The Tracey Ullman Show in [1987](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/History_of_The_Simpsons>). As the longtime Marge Simpson voice actor, Julie Kavner has continued to bring the character to life in every season of The Simpsons. She even won an Emmy Award for her outstanding work. Known for keeping her life private, Kavner rarely appears in public as Marge. Her dedication and unique acting style have shaped the character for many years.

### Julie Kavner’s Role

Julie Kavner is the first [Marge Simpson voice actor](<https://dubbingai.io/blog/simpsons-voice-actors-2025/>). She [started voicing Marge in 1987](<https://simpsonsfanon.fandom.com/wiki/Julie_Kavner>). The Tracey Ullman Show showed the first Simpsons cartoons. Producers picked Kavner because her voice sounded special and she was funny. She did not have to try out for the part. She already worked on The Tracey Ullman Show as a regular. This choice made her the only English-speaking Marge Simpson voice since the start.

Kavner’s way of playing Marge is different from other voice actors. She gives Marge a rough, caring, and easy-to-know voice. Many fans think her acting makes Marge feel warm and real. She has always voiced Marge in every Simpsons episode. Her hard work helped make Marge one of TV’s favorite cartoon moms.

Julie Kavner likes to keep her life private. She almost never goes out as Marge Simpson or joins big events. She wants her work to show who she is. This makes her a bit of a mystery to fans. It also makes Marge more interesting. Kavner’s wish for privacy means she almost never does Marge’s voice outside the studio.

### Emmy Award and Recognition

Julie Kavner’s job as the Marge Simpson voice actor has won her praise. In 1992, she got the [Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Voice-Over Performance](<https://the-jh-movie-collection-official.fandom.com/wiki/Julie_Kavner>). She won for her work in the season three episode “I Married Marge.” This episode showed Marge’s early life with Homer. It also showed Kavner’s strong feelings as Marge Simpson’s voice.

Kavner’s Emmy win was a big moment for her and The Simpsons. Before this, she got a few [Emmy nominations for The Tracey Ullman Show from 1987 to 1989](<https://www.televisionacademy.com/bios/julie-kavner>). Her skill and hard work made her respected by other voice actors. Many fans and critics think her Marge Simpson is one of TV’s best.

### Patty and Selma

Patty and Selma Bouvier are Marge’s older twin sisters. They work at the Springfield Department of Motor Vehicles. The sisters act tough most of the time. [Julie Kavner voices both Patty and Selma](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Patty_and_Selma>). She gives each sister a special sound. [Patty’s voice is lower and sounds more like a man](<https://ultimatepopculture.fandom.com/wiki/Selma_Bouvier>). Selma’s voice is a little sweeter. Matt Groening wanted them to sound like people who drain energy. Al Jean said Kavner changes her voice for each sister. This makes it easy to know who is talking. Kavner’s skill shows she knows her characters well.

> Julie Kavner can make two voices for the twins. This makes the show better. Fans always know which sister is talking, even if they do not look.

### Jacqueline Bouvier

Jacqueline Bouvier is Marge’s mother. She is in several episodes and acts more serious. Julie Kavner also voices Marge’s mom. She uses a softer and older-sounding voice for her. This helps Jacqueline stand out from her daughters. Wikipedia and other sources say Kavner voices Marge, Patty, Selma, and Jacqueline. Her work gives each family member their own style.

[Julie Kavner’s talent](<https://dubbingai.io/blog/simpsons-voice-actors-2025/>) as a voice actor helps The Simpsons feel real. She voices Marge and her family. Each character is easy to remember. Her work is a big reason she is so important to the show.
